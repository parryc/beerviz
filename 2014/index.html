Beers I Have Known and Loved
2014

§Introduction

   So you're actually reading this. You aren't sick of me talking about beer all day everyday forever. ... Cool. Some people do music, some people do events, I'm gonna talk about all the beer I tried this year.



❧Things You Should Know

   I've ignored counts of less than 5 in the superlatives. That is, best/worst brewery (for example) only includes breweries that I tried more than 5 times this year.

   If there's a percentage in the parentheses, it's an ABV. If there's no percentage, it's a rating.

   I use a 5 point rating scale:

  • Bad (0-1)
  • Meh (1-2)
  • Ok (2-3)
  • Good (3-4)
  • Great (4-5)
